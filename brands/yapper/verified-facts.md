# Verified Facts — Yapper

> Per-brand claim registry. Every stat, date, and regulatory claim used in social content lives here with source + verified_date. HARD GATE: no post contains an unverified claim.

## Sector rules

- **Sector:** consumer_health
- **Regulatory body:** FDA/FTC (US), state health departments
- **Hard-violation list:**
  - Any claim that Yapper diagnoses, treats, cures, or prevents any medical condition
  - Any claim that Yapper is a medical device
  - Any claim that Yapper can prevent suicide, self-harm, or medical emergencies
  - Any claim that Yapper replaces clinical judgment or professional care
  - Any clinical outcome claims (e.g., "reduces depression by X%")
- **Soft-violation list:**
  - Implying Yapper is therapeutic without disclaimer
  - Using "patient" instead of "resident"
  - Framing loneliness as something Yapper "solves" vs "addresses"
  - Omitting the medical disclaimer when discussing health/wellbeing/memory
- **Verification requirements:** Every stat needs source URL + date accessed + author/publisher. Stats expire after 6 months. Regulatory claims need re-verification annually.
