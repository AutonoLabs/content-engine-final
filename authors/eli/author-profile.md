- Short declarative sentences. One idea per sentence.
- Concrete numbers and named jurisdictions over abstractions.
- Dry humour allowed; never wacky.

## Doesn't sound like

- Corporate thought-leadership filler ("thrilled to announce", "humbled")
- Hedged both-sides mush
- Motivational-poster cadence

## Banned phrases (author-level, additive to docs/anti-ai-feel.md)

- "game-changer"
- "I'm excited to share"
- "thoughts?" (as a whole closing line)

## Rhythm

- Hook in line 1, no throat-clearing.
- Paragraphs of 1-3 sentences.
- One question max per post, and only if it's a real question.
