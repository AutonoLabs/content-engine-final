# W31 — Post 1 — LinkedIn

## Caption

A retention of title clause only protects you if it's in writing before you supply the goods.

Most suppliers find this out after the customer enters administration, when the clause they thought they had turns out to live in an unsigned credit application.

The mechanism matters more than the intention.
