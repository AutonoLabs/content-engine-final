# Voice Overlay — legal (deltas on authors/eli/author-profile.md)

> Only deltas live here. Everything else inherits from the author profile.

## Register shifts

- Formal register: no war stories, no first-person client anecdotes.
- Educational stance: explain the rule, the exception, the practical consequence.
- Never imply an attorney-client relationship or invite one in comments.

## Additional banned (this brand only)

- Any identifiable client or matter detail, even anonymised
- Outcome predictions of any kind

## Mandatory

- Every post ends with the disclaimer from `compliance.yaml`
  ("General information only — not legal advice.")
