## Caption

A snagging list is the moment the builder stops being the hero.

The brickwork is up. The kitchen's fitted. The extension's plumbed. The client signs off on the milestones, the money releases, the trades leave. Everyone goes home thinking the job's done.

Then someone walks back through with a torch and a notebook. The hinge on the cupboard door sticks. The skirting board has a 4mm gap. The silicone seal around the bath is uneven. The handle on the bifold needs a quarter-turn adjustment.

The snagging list is where the actual quality of the work shows up.

A good builder welcomes the snagging list. A great builder writes it themselves before the client does. A bad builder ghosts when the 5% retention is the only thing pulling them back to site.

We built retention release into the platform specifically so the snagging moment has a deadline. The list gets signed. The retention releases. The job actually closes.

Boring. Important.

—

What does your snagging process look like right now?
