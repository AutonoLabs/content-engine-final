### Example: AllSquared

**Brand basics**
- Brand name: allsquared
- Tagline: Builders keep getting paid.
- Founded: 2024
- HQ: London
- Website: allsquared.uk
- Sector: fintech

**What you do (1-3 sentences)**
We make milestone-based escrow for UK construction project work — kitchens, extensions, agency retainers. £20k-£200k range. FCA-regulated, audited, milestone by milestone.

**Product**
AllSquared holds client money in FCA-regulated escrow and releases it to the builder when each milestone is signed off. The platform replaces the awkward "trust me with £50k for 8 weeks" phase of project work. Built specifically for UK construction: handles JCT contracts, retention, snagging, and CIS where relevant.

**Target audience**
- Primary ICP: UK construction project owners (kitchens, extensions, refurbs) + agencies + property developers, commissioning £20k-£200k
- Demographics: 35-65, mixed gender, UK, household income £60k+, building for themselves or as part of portfolio
- Psychographics: They've been burned before. They distrust builders who demand 50% upfront. They want protection without slowing the project down. They are skeptical of "fintech" branding but trust FCA-regulated services.
- How they find you: organic search (Google), referral from architect/tradesperson, LinkedIn (for agencies), word of mouth

**Tone — sounds like**
Founder-to-founder. Peer-level. UK tradesperson register when talking to builders; professional reassurance when talking to homeowners/agency clients. Premium without being snobby. Direct. Specific. Uses trade vocabulary naturally (snagging, retention, JCT, day rate, making good).

**Tone — doesn't sound like**
Corporate. B2B SaaS-y. Fintech brochure. No "empower", no "unlock", no "transform your experience". Doesn't lecture. Doesn't moralise. Doesn't promise miracles.

**Banned phrases (initial)**
- peace of mind
- trusted by thousands
- revolutionize
- game-changing
- disruption
- your dream project
- stress-free

**Founder voice samples**
> "Builders keep getting paid only when the work's done. FCA-regulated escrow, milestone by milestone. No games."
> "We don't replace the contract. We replace the bit where you're wondering if you'll see your £50k again."

**Existing assets**

**Launch target date:** rolling

**Notes**
FCA-authorised-adjacent — public claims about escrow need to comply with UK financial promotion rules. We're allowed to make factual claims; we avoid implied guarantees, "your money is safe," unbalanced benefit language. Risk: clients may misunderstand what escrow protects against (project failure vs payment default).
