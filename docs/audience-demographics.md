# Audience Demographics — Who Uses Each Platform

> General user demographic knowledge per platform. Independent of any specific brand/project. Helps calibrate voice, tone, complexity, and topic selection. Updated 2026-07-07 (data from 2025-2026 sources).

---

## Why this doc exists

Different platforms = different audiences. A 25-year-old TikTok user and a 45-year-old LinkedIn user consume content differently. They want different things. The voice, hook, topic, and complexity all need to match who's on the other side.

This doc is the canonical reference. Every platform prompt inherits audience context from here. Use it to calibrate: how casual should the tone be, how complex can the topic be, what ages are we writing for, what intent (B2B or consumer).

## LinkedIn (B2B, professional)

**Age distribution (global, 2025):**
- 25-34: ~60% (largest segment, 47.3% in 2025 data)
- 18-24: ~24-28.7% (Gen Z growing fast)
- 35-54: ~19-20.7%
- 55+: ~3% (small minority)

**Gender:** 56.4-56.9% male, 43.1-43.6% female.

**Geography:**
- US: ~257M users
- India: ~161.5M
- Brazil: ~83M
- UK: ~47.5M
- EMEA: 407M+ (largest region)

**Income:** 54% of US users earn >$100k/year.

**Intent:** B2B primarily. 65M decision-makers, 10M C-level execs use LinkedIn.

**Voice calibration:**
- Authoritative but conversational
- Founder-to-peer (not corporate-to-customer)
- Industry-specific language acceptable
- Business outcomes, regulatory clarity, financial accuracy valued
- Longer captions OK (algorithm rewards depth)
- Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10am (UK) best times

## X / Twitter (news, B2B + consumer)

**Age distribution (global, 2025):**
- 25-34: ~37.5% (largest)
- 18-24: ~32%
- 35-49: ~21%
- 50+: ~9%
- 70% of users are 18-34 (most youth-skewed)

**Gender:** 63-64.4% male, 36-37% female. Most male-skewed major platform.

**Geography:**
- US: ~111M users, 22.67% of unique visitors
- Japan: ~71M
- Indonesia: ~22.9M
- India: ~22.2M
- UK: ~19M

**Income:** Mixed. Heavy B2B adoption (82% of B2B marketers use it). 61% of journalists use X daily.

**Intent:** News (59.7%) + brand research (37.8%) + entertainment (35.4%). Strong B2B utility despite consumer-led surface.

**Voice calibration:**
- Terse, punchy, contrarian ok
- Threads work well for storytelling
- 1 idea per tweet
- Reply-bait acceptable (replies > retweets > likes)
- Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10am + 5-7pm (UK) best times

## Instagram (consumer, shopping)

**Age distribution (global, 2025):**
- 25-34: 31.6-33.3% (largest)
- 18-24: 29.5-31.6%
- 35-44: ~16-17%
- 45+: ~17% (declining)
- 61% under 35

**Gender:** Global 52.5% male, 46.5% female. **US specifically: 55% female, 45% male.**

**Geography:**
- India: 350-413M users (~12-14% of global)
- US: 169-176M
- Brazil: 134-140M

**Income:** 60% of US adult users earn >$100k/year.

**Intent:** 70% shop/browse, 81% research brands, 61% discover products, 29% buy directly. **B2B activity minimal.**

**Voice calibration:**
- Visual first, caption second
- Aesthetic matters (color, lighting, composition)
- Lifestyle language ok
- 1-3 emojis per caption max
- Hashtags at end of caption (5-10)
- Tuesday-Thursday, 11am-1pm + 7-9pm (UK) best times

## TikTok (consumer, discovery)

**Age distribution (global, 2026):**
- 25-34: 35.3% (largest)
- 18-24: 30.7%
- 35-44: 16.4%
- 45-54: 9.2%
- 55+: 8.4%
- 40+ is fastest-growing segment
- US teens (10-19) = 25% of US users

**Gender:** Global 54.6-55.7% male, 45.2-46.5% female. **US specifically: 61% female, 39% male.**

**Geography:**
- Indonesia: 157-180M
- US: 136-150M
- Brazil: ~105M
- Mexico: ~77M
- Vietnam: ~67M

**Income:** 37% of US users earn >$100k household.

**Intent:** **Consumer-focused B2C.** 55% of Gen Z prefer TikTok for product discovery. 82% of Gen Z have accounts.

**Voice calibration:**
- Casual, native, conversational
- Lowercase ok, fragments ok
- Fast cuts, hook in first 0.5s
- Trending sounds boost reach
- 3-5 hashtags max
- Tuesday-Thursday + Sunday, 7-9am + 7-11pm (UK) best times

## YouTube (consumer + education)

**Age distribution (global, 2026):**
- 25-34: 21.3% (largest)
- 35-44: 18.7%
- 25-44 combined: ~40%
- 18-24: 15.8%
- 55+: 19.4% (significant older cohort)
- Average user age: 35-44

**Gender:** 54.3% male, 45.7% female globally. **US specifically: 51.2% female, 48.8% male.**

**Geography:**
- India: 518M (largest)
- US: 253-259M
- Brazil: 144-147M
- Indonesia: 139-143M
- Mexico: 83.6M

**Income:** 90% of users earning >$100k are active. 90% of college graduates active.

**Intent:** Mixed. 76% of consumers on YT. 35% of US adults get news there. Professional education + product reviews = strong B2B utility despite consumer surface.

**Voice calibration:**
- Authoritative + accessible
- Longer explanations ok
- SEO titles matter
- Description matters (search + recommendation)
- Tags help discoverability
- Friday-Sunday, 12-3pm + 7-10pm (UK) best times

## Threads (Meta, consumer, casual)

**Age distribution (2025-2026):**
- 25-34: 28.75-36.4% (largest)
- 18-24: 20.36-37%
- 35-44: 19.15%
- Gen Z & Millennials: 17% adoption each
- Gen X: 11%
- Boomers: 5%

**Gender:** 57.85-68% male, 32-42% female. Male-leaning.

**Geography:**
- Armenia: 48.26% of users (all desktop — likely bot traffic)
- US: 17.32% (mobile)
- Germany: 7.91%
- India: ~20% of downloads
- 400M MAU global, 143-150M DAU

**Intent:** Consumer casual. 70% of US daily Threads users also use Facebook.

**Voice calibration:**
- Most casual of all platforms
- Like talking to a smart friend
- Short posts (300-500 chars)
- No hashtags (algo doesn't weight them)
- Multi-post threads work for storytelling
- Wednesday-Friday, 8-10am + 12-2pm (UK) best times

- Gen Z (18-24): ~20%

**Gender:** 51% female, 49% male (nearly balanced).

- Older demographic tolerance for slower pacing
- Audience overlaps with Threads heavily

## Cross-platform audience insights

**Three platform clusters:**

1. **B2B + Professional:** LinkedIn (primary), X (secondary)
   - Age: 25-45
   - Voice: authoritative, peer-to-peer, business-relevant
   - Topics: industry, regulation, professional growth, building

2. **Consumer + Younger:** TikTok (primary), Instagram (secondary)
   - Age: 18-34 (TikTok slightly younger, IG slightly broader)
   - Voice: casual, visual, lifestyle-oriented
   - Topics: discovery, products, trends, behind-the-scenes

3. **Mixed + Broad:** YouTube (primary), Threads (casual), Facebook (older)
   - YouTube: broad age, education + entertainment
   - Threads: younger but casual, like X but less news-driven
   - Facebook: older, community

**UK audience bias:** All platforms have UK audiences, but UK-specific reach varies:
- LinkedIn: 47.5M users (strong)
- X: ~19M (moderate)
- Instagram: significant UK presence
- TikTok: strong UK teen/young adult
- YouTube: strong UK presence
- Threads: significant UK early adopters
- Facebook: strong but older skew

For Autono Labs brands targeting UK, default to: LinkedIn (business), X (news + thought leadership), Instagram (visual storytelling), TikTok (discovery), YouTube (depth).
